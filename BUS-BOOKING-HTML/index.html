ABOUT US

This is a bus company offering world-class transportation and courier services in an innovative way while harnessing the use of technology. It is registered within the East African region.

CORE VALUES

  1. Policies for improved customer service and campaigns to encourage the use of the bus
  2. Continuous improvement of our services as regards the number of routes offered, journey times, punctuality and regularity, safety and comfort
  3. Achieving excellence in the services offered
  4. We strive to ensure compliance with all prevailing standards and laws concerning the environment, and we aim to do no more by taking a proactive attitude
  5. Continuous improvement aimed at adopting preventive measures rather than corrective ones

VISION

Aims at being a leader in mobility in our region, in relation to the services offered and their quality. We will be deploying a variety of measures with an aim to improving the image society has of our company and in order to ensure it is socially active and responsible as well as promote the development of the city, the environment, customers, employees, and the rest of the actors involved.

MISSION

To provide the public transport service to travelers in the region with our continuous effort to be leaders and offer a competitive service. We aim at satisfying the mobility needs of current and potential users, with top-quality services and practical information, with competitive costs, and contributing to the sustainability of our environment.
